Community Guidelines

Welcome to our community! We're thrilled to have you here. To ensure everyone has a positive and enjoyable experience, we've put together some guidelines. Please take a moment to read through them.

Don't be a Jerk

It's so easy to be uncivil given the anonymity of the web. Just don't.

  • Treat everyone with kindness and respect. We all come from different backgrounds and have unique perspectives. Let's engage in thoughtful, respectful discussions.
  • When providing feedback or criticism, do so constructively. Aim to help others improve and grow, rather than tearing them down.
  • Bullying or harassment of any kind is forbidden. This includes offensive comments related to immutable characteristics of individuals.
  • Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent.
  • Avoid posting spam or excessive self-promotion. While we encourage sharing your work and achievements, please do so in a way that adds value to the community.
  • Never post links to "adult content" without categorizing it as NSFW (Not Safe For Work).
  • Never post anything that violates (your) local, (your) state, or federal law.
  • Keep conversations relevant to the topic at hand. This helps maintain a focused and productive environment for everyone. For example, don't post political views in non-political channels.

Report Issues

If you see something that violates these guidelines, please report it to our moderation team using the tools provided in the feed where you discover it. We're here to help maintain a safe and welcoming environment for everyone.

Have Fun!

Last but not least, have fun! This community is a place to learn, share, and grow together. Enjoy your time here and make the most of the connections you build.

Thank you for being a part of our community. Let's work together to keep it friendly, supportive, and inspiring!

Stay curious, stay organized, and happy linking!
