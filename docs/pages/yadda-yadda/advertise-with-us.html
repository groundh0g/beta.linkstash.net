Dear Potential Advertisers,

Thank you for your interest in partnering with Linkstash! We're excited about the possibility of working together in the future. As a growing platform, we're currently focused on building our community and enhancing our features. At this time, we're not accepting advertisements, but we're diligently collecting analytics and tracking data to prepare for the day when we can officially support ads.

For now, you'll notice that all ads on our site are simple placeholders directing visitors to friends of the site. This helps us ensure that the infrastructure is in place and ready to roll out when the time comes.

If you're still interested in advertising before the official release of ads on Linkstash, contact us.

We appreciate your patience and understanding as we continue to grow. Stay tuned for updates, and we look forward to collaborating with you soon!

Warm regards,
The Linkstash Team
